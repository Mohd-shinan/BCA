About Us

Our BCA department offers cutting-edge education in computer applications, fostering a deep understanding of the IT field.

Department of Computer Science offers a three-year professional UG programme (BCA). The department commenced its operation in 2023, right from the inception of the college. The department offers programmes to equip undergraduates to be competent in the IT economy. The department enables learners to think critically in the course of digital technology.

Bachelors in Computer Application (BCA) is a three-year undergraduate degree course for students who wish to delve into the world of Computer languages. The BCA course is one of the most popular options to get started with a career in Information Technology.

A degree in BCA is at par with a B.Tech/B.E degree in Computer Science or Information Technology. For a BCA aspirant, this degree helps in setting up a sound academic base for an advanced career in Computer Applications.

The IT industry is growing rapidly and with this, the demand for BCA graduates, too, is increasing every passing day. After obtaining a BCA degree, students can find lucrative job opportunities in leading IT companies such as Oracle, IBM, Infosys, and Wipro. Some of the roles that one can bag after completing a BCA programme are that of a System Engineer, Software Tester, Junior Programmer, Web Developer, System Administrator, Software Developer, etc.

BCA graduates are not only recruited by the private sector but also by public sector organisations. Government organisations like the Indian Air Force (IAF), Indian Army, and Indian Navy also hire a large bunch of computer professionals for their IT departments.
