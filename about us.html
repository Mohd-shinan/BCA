Overview

The BCA Department offers a comprehensive program designed to prepare students for success in the IT industry with a focus on practical skills and theoretical knowledge.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Academic Qualifications: Completion of higher secondary education (12th grade) or equivalent.
  • Minimum Marks: 50% aggregate in qualifying exams.
  • Entrance Exam: May be required depending on the institution.

Facilities

  • Computer Labs: Modern PCs, high-speed internet, and the latest software for hands-on learning.
  • Software Tools: Access to various IDEs, database management systems, and development tools.
  • Library: Extensive collection of books, journals, and online resources.
  • Multimedia Facilities: Tools for digital content creation and management.

Placement and Career Support

  • Industry Connections: Regular career fairs and recruitment drives with top IT companies.
  • Internships: Opportunities for real-world experience with leading firms.
  • Career Counseling: Guidance on job search, resume building, and interviews.
  • Alumni Network: Mentorship and networking with successful graduates.
  • Skill Development: Workshops and training on emerging technologies and soft skills.
